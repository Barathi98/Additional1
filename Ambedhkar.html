Rationalism

Ambedhkar-Democracy is not merely a form of government. It is primarily a mode of associated living, of conjoint communicated experience. It is essentialy an attitude of respect and reverence towards our fellow men.

Periyar-Man is equal to man, there should not be exploitation. One should help the other. No one should harm anybody. Generally there should be no room for grievance or complaint from anybody. Everyone should live and let others live, with a national spirit.
